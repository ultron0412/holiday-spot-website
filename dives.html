Explore the Underwater World

Discover world-class diving sites with vibrant coral reefs, exotic marine life, and unforgettable underwater adventures.

  • Coral Garden

    The Coral Garden is a vibrant reef teeming with colorful fish and exotic marine life. It is perfect for both beginners and experienced divers. Explore intricate coral formations and encounter tropical fish species in their natural habitat.

    Depth: 5m - 20m | Visibility: 30m+ | Best For: All Levels

  • Manta Point

    Famous for the graceful Manta Rays that visit the cleaning stations, this site offers an unforgettable experience for nature lovers. Witness these magnificent creatures gliding through the water in their natural environment. An encounter with mantas is truly a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

    Depth: 10m - 25m | Best Season: December - April | Best For: Experienced Divers
