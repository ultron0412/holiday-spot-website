Taste of the Maldives

Indulge in world-class cuisine featuring fresh seafood, international delicacies, and authentic Maldivian flavors.

  • Fresh Seafood Platter

    Enjoy the catch of the day, grilled to perfection with local spices and served with a side of tropical salad. Our chefs source the freshest ingredients from local fishermen to ensure the highest quality and authentic flavors.

    Category: Seafood | Cuisine: Maldivian

  • Traditional Maldivian Breakfast

    Start your day with Mas Huni, a delicious mixture of tuna, onion, coconut, and chili, served with Roshi flatbread. This traditional breakfast dish is a staple in Maldivian cuisine and provides the perfect energy boost for your island adventures.

    Category: Breakfast | Cuisine: Traditional Maldivian

  • Tropical Fruit Medley

    A refreshing selection of seasonal tropical fruits including papaya, mango, and passion fruit. Perfectly chilled and beautifully presented, this dessert is the ideal way to end your meal while enjoying the tropical climate.

    Category: Dessert | Cuisine: Tropical
